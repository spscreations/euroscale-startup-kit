## The Narrative

### Why Now?

Three forces converge to create a once-in-a-decade opportunity:

**1. The Sovereign Cloud Tipping Point.** The EU sovereign cloud market is $154.7B in 2025, growing at 27% CAGR to $824B by 2032. AWS itself is investing €7.8B in a "European Sovereign Cloud" — proving demand is massive. But AWS's answer is still AWS. European companies want *genuinely independent* European infrastructure, not an AWS subsidiary labeled "sovereign."

**2. The PlanetScale Gap.** PlanetScale proved developers *love* database branching and serverless MySQL. They reached 700%+ user growth and a $5B valuation. But they're US-based, have no European data centers, and their pricing (at AWS margins) is painful for European startups paying in euros. When they killed their free hobby tier, developers fled — but had nowhere European to go.

**3. The Hetzner Opportunity.** Hetzner has 400K+ customers, 28 years of infrastructure experience, and pricing that's 60–80% cheaper than AWS. But they don't offer managed databases or a PlanetScale-class service. The infrastructure exists — the software layer doesn't. That's our gap.

### Why Us?

We are building the missing layer on top of European cloud infrastructure:

- **Proven OSS stack** — Vitess (YouTube, GitHub), Citus (Microsoft), Kubernetes (all of cloud-native) — no unproven technology
- **K8s-native by design** — Operators, CRDs, GitOps. Works the way modern platform teams want to work
- **Multi-cloud from day one** — Hetzner, OVH, Exoscale. Not locked to one provider
- **Cost structure that wins** — 3–5× cheaper infra costs than AWS-based competitors. Better margins at lower prices
- **GDPR-native architecture** — Not a checkbox compliance add-on. Data never leaves EU borders. Gaia-X ready
